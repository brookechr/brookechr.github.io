Undergraduate Teaching Assistant for Discrete Math

This is my second semester working as an undergraduate teaching assistant for Georgia Tech's College of Computing. I hold virtual office hours for 3 hours a week, answer about 10 student's questions a day on an online forum, grade 20-25 students homework's a week, attend lecture and answer students questions in a live chat, and occasionally record recitation videos to be viewed by all of the almost 500 students in the course. I also attend weekly meetings and communicate frequently with the head TA and professors.

When we return to in-person classes, I will assume more responsibilities, including holding weekly recitations for about 30 students, helping to create, proctor, and grade exams, and helping teach lecture in the absence of the professor.
